    Transitional & Emergency Services


    Every business needs a backup plan when their systems are down, going through an upgrade transition, or are currently overwhelmed. ByFone offers a Transitional Service that aids your company during these times.     We’ll be your customers’ point of contact when your business is impacted by upgrades, transitions, shutdowns, or different emergencies like weather-related shutdowns, holidays, or global pandemics such as COVID-19. When faced with a workforce reduction we have your customer service needs covered for a few days, a few weeks, or for a few months as your team recovers.
